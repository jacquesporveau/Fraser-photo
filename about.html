Fraser Evans

Who I am

I'm an artist from the global village and my art is photography.

My father started with photography back in the 1960's as a teenager when he bought his first camera. He took many photos with me when I was growing up and as a result, stopping to set up the tripod became a routine on our hikes.

Through exploration of his 35mm cameras and with my first digital camera in junior high I've developed a passion for documenting the real and surreal. Please contact me with questions about my services including portrait, wedding and commercial photo services.
